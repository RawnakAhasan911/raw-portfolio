Algae Bio-Reactor

Research-oriented project focused on pollution control using algae to absorb CO₂ and purify industrial air.

Tech Stack:
Environmental Science Biotechnology Bio-medical Engineering
Role:

Technical Lead & Presenter

Proposed and led the design of a Chlorella vulgaris-based algae bio-reactor to absorb CO₂ and industrial pollutants, enhancing air quality in Dhaka’s industrial zones. Planned its prototype, scalability, and performance analysis.

The project received recognition for its originality, potential environmental impact, and technical depth at the Anwar Group Presents IPE Carnivesta event.

Line-Following Robot

An IR Sensor-based robot built using Arduino with advanced sensor calibration logic for precise line tracking.

Tech Stack:
Arduino C++ Electronics
Role:

Lead Developer & Hardware Engineer

Designed and built an IR sensor-based line-following robot using Arduino microcontroller. The project involved sensor integration, motor control, and developing a sophisticated calibration algorithm for accurate line detection.

The robot successfully navigated complex paths with sharp turns and varying line widths, demonstrating the effectiveness of the custom calibration logic implemented.
